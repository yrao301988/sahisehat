Ayushman Bharat (PM-JAY)

Last date to apply: Open Enrollment

Benefits:

Provides a cashless health cover of ₹5 lakh per family per year for secondary and tertiary care hospitalization across public and empaneled private hospitals.

Eligibility Criteria:

  • Families listed in the SECC 2011 database.
  • Lack of a pucca house or regular salaried job.
  • Belongs to designated vulnerable socio-economic categories.
State Scheme • Eligible

State Jan Arogya Yojana

Last date to apply: 30th Nov

Benefits:

Provides comprehensive medical coverage for identified severe illnesses requiring hospitalization, surgeries, and therapies.

Eligibility Criteria:

  • Resident of the state holding a valid ration card.
  • Family income below state-defined poverty line threshold.
